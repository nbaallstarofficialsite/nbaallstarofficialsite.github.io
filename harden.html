James Edward Harden Jr. (born August 26, 1989) is an American professional
  basketball player for the Houston Rockets of the National Basketball
Association (NBA). He played college basketball for Arizona State, where
he was named a consensus All-American and Pac-10 Player of the Year in 2009.
Harden was selected with the third overall pick in the 2009 NBA draft by
the Oklahoma City Thunder. In 2012, he was named NBA Sixth Man of the Year 
with the Thunder and helped the team reach the NBA Finals, where they lost
to the Miami Heat in five games.
